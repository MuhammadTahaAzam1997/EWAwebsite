Surf Excel And Its Ideology



A Company Known By Its Brands
An FMCG giant known by its innovation and vast variety of product category Uniliver, has been in Pakistan since the 1948, producing and catering to millions of consumers daily has a past, a journey less known to people, a long forgotten message, that should've been insulated into the minds of consumers.
Among many other brands Surf excel is one of the premium and highly successful brand of Uniliver, it started its operations in 1948 and is catering to millions of households, besides watching many tvc ads of the brand still many households are unfamiliar with the brand message that Surf excel wants to communicate.
Surf excel always portrayed a strong message through its commercials, for instance one of its ad states the growing stage of life where your child spreads his/her wings out into the world. The ad revolves around the concept of sharing, unlike adults kids are very fond of sharing their belongings without any hassle, which enables them to face the world or which makes them ready for the available challenges.
The central theme of the brand is "Daag Tou Ache Hain" this idea is not just to sell the product, but to signify the importance of learning and getting to know the world. Children go out and play with their friends that allows them to witness the world through their naked eyes, this cognitive process boosts the learning process within the child.
Such kind of ideology not just helps the brand to achieve its sales targets but also stimulates the cognitive process of learning and exploring the world. The part of a world we live in, is such a suffocative place for kids that their creativity doesn't grow, or if it grows the intensity is on such a low scale that it becomes difficult for a kid to compete.
Thanks to Surf excel who gave us the platform to unleash the creativity and growth for our upcoming generations.
